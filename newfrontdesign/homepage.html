Yuzu-Glazed Salmon with Charred Asparagus

Preparation Steps

1

Pat the salmon fillets dry with paper towels. Season generously with sea salt and a pinch of white pepper. Let rest at room temperature for 10 minutes to ensure even cooking.

2

Whisk together the yuzu juice, organic honey, and a dash of tamari. Reduce in a small saucepan over medium heat until it achieves a syrupy consistency.

3

Heat a cast-iron skillet with a touch of avocado oil. Sear the salmon skin-side down for 4 minutes until crispy. Flip and glaze immediately with the yuzu reduction.
